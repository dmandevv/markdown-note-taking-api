## Text Formatting

*   To make text *italic*, use single asterisks or underscores (`*italic*` or `_italic_`).
*   To make text **bold**, use double asterisks or underscores (`**bold**` or `__bold__`).
*   To make text ***bold and italic***, use three asterisks (`***bold and italic***`).
*   To ignore formatting characters, use a backslash before the character, like so: `\*not italicized\*`.
